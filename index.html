What it's built on

The principles behind
BrandEngine

Every post BrandEngine writes is shaped by these principles — grounded in UK advertising law, real audience intelligence, and one simple goal: grow your business.

The golden rule

Every post must be truthful, make the client look good, give customers a reason to engage or buy, and leave both sides happy.

Principle 1 — Truth & compliance
01
Always tell the truth
Only say what can be stood behind. Never invent claims, stats or details not in the brief.
02
UK ASA CAP Code
Every post checked against UK advertising law — substantiation, testimonials, pricing, food claims.
03
Never hurt the client
No promises the client can't keep. No legal risk. No embarrassing mistakes. When in doubt — leave it out.
Principle 2 — Audience & intelligence
04
Audience intelligence
Knows who your customers are, when they're online, and what triggers them to buy.
05
Timing intelligence
Posts at the optimal time — lunch peaks, evening cravings, payday weekends.
06
Local context
Weather, festivals, sports nights, school holidays — content that feels timely and local.
07
Campaign calendar
Eid, Christmas, Diwali, Premier League — campaign briefs suggested automatically ahead of time.
Principle 3 — Content & engagement
08
Brand awareness
Consistent voice, colours and tagline so customers recognise you instantly.
09
Drive engagement
Questions, polls, UGC — every post invites a response, comment or share.
10
Be informative
Hours, menu, offers, new items. Customers who know more, buy more.
11
Build trust
Hygiene ratings, reviews, years in business — credibility that converts browsers to buyers.
Principle 4 — Conversion & growth
12
Right CTA every time
Order Now, WhatsApp, Google Review — BrandEngine picks the right call to action per post type.
13
Mutually beneficial
Every post creates value for both client and customer. Never a one-sided hard sell.
14
Performance learning
Tracks what drives orders. Does more of what wins. Gets smarter every week.
Principle 5 — Quality & brand consistency
15
Quality gate on every post
Five checks before anything goes live — tone, truth, accuracy, CTA, brand fit. Nothing publishes without your approval.
16
Polite, courteous, human
Warmth in every word. Thank customers. Acknowledge occasions. Be the business the community is proud of.
