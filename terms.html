Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Meta API

By granting access, you authorise CNR Digital Ltd to publish posts, images, and updates to your Facebook Pages and connected Instagram Business accounts, and to retrieve basic page metrics for reporting. You can revoke this access at any time via Facebook Settings → Business Integrations or Meta Business Suite.

Google Business Profile Google API

By granting access, you authorise CNR Digital Ltd to publish posts and updates to your Google Business Profile listing and to retrieve basic performance data for reporting. You can revoke this access at any time via Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access.
