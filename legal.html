About CNR Digital Ltd

CNR Digital Ltd is a social media management company incorporated in England and Wales. We provide daily social media posting, content creation, and platform management services to small businesses in the United Kingdom and India.

Our registered office is at 2 Coppidwell Drive, Aylesbury, England, HP21 9QF. The company is registered at Companies House under number 17185406.

02

Platform Authorisations

CNR Digital Ltd operates API integrations with Meta (Facebook and Instagram), Google, LinkedIn, and other social platforms on behalf of clients. All platform access is granted by clients via official OAuth authorisation flows and may be revoked at any time through each platform's settings.

CNR Digital Ltd is the legal entity named in all API developer applications and Business Verification submissions to Meta, Google, and other platform operators.
